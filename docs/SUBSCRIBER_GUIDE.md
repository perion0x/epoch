## Getting Started

### Do I Need a Wallet?

**For Free Content:** No wallet required!
- Browse and read free newsletters without connecting a wallet
- No registration or sign-up needed

**For Premium Content:** Yes, you'll need a Sui wallet
- Required to prove NFT ownership
- Needed to decrypt premium sections
- Free to set up

### Setting Up a Wallet (Optional)

If you want to access premium content:

1. **Choose a Wallet:**
   - Sui Wallet (official)
   - Suiet
   - Ethos Wallet
   - Any Sui-compatible wallet

2. **Install the Wallet:**
   - Download from official website or app store
   - Follow wallet's setup instructions
   - **Important:** Save your recovery phrase securely!

3. **Get Some SUI (Optional):**
   - You only need SUI for transactions (subscribing, buying NFTs)
   - Reading content is free (no gas fees)
   - Get SUI from exchanges or faucets (testnet)

### Connecting Your Wallet

1. Visit the platform
2. Click "Connect Wallet" in the top right
3. Select your wallet provider
4. Approve the connection
5. Your address will appear in the top right

## Accessing Premium Content

### Prerequisites

1. Connected wallet
2. Access NFT for the newsletter in your wallet
3. Internet connection

### Unlocking Premium Content

1. **Navigate to Issue:**
   - Go to an issue with premium content
   - You'll see locked sections marked with ðŸ”’

2. **Check Your Access:**
   - If you own the access NFT, you'll see "Unlock Premium" button
   - If you don't own the NFT, you'll see "Get Access" button

3. **Unlock Content:**
   - Click "Unlock Premium"
   - The platform verifies your NFT ownership
   - Approve the Seal verification transaction (no gas fee)
   - Premium content decrypts automatically
   - Read the full content!

### How It Works

The platform uses cryptographic access control:

1. **Verification:** Your NFT ownership is verified on-chain
2. **Approval:** Seal key servers approve your access
3. **Decryption:** Content decrypts in your browser
4. **Privacy:** Decryption happens locally, content never sent to servers

**Important:** Decryption keys are temporary and never stored. You may need to unlock again in a new session.

## Managing Subscriptions

### Following Newsletters

Stay updated on your favorite newsletters:

1. **Subscribe:**
   - Go to a newsletter page
   - Click "Subscribe" button
   - Approve the transaction (small gas fee)
   - You're now subscribed!

2. **View Subscriptions:**
   - Click "My Subscriptions" in navigation
   - See all newsletters you follow
   - Quick access to new issues

3. **Unsubscribe:**
   - Go to newsletter page
   - Click "Unsubscribe"
   - Approve the transaction
   - Subscription removed

### Subscription vs. Access

**Important Distinction:**

**Subscription (Following):**
- Free to do
- Helps you track newsletters
- Enables notifications (if available)
- Does NOT grant access to premium content

**Access NFT:**
- May require purchase
- Grants access to premium content
- Transferable
- Can be sold or traded

You can subscribe without having an access NFT, and vice versa.

## Acquiring Access NFTs

### Methods to Get Access NFTs

#### 1. Direct from Creator

- Contact the creator directly
- Arrange payment off-chain
- Creator mints NFT to your address
- Most common method

#### 2. NFT Marketplaces

- Check Sui NFT marketplaces
- Search for the newsletter's collection
- Purchase listed NFTs
- Transfer to your wallet

#### 3. Airdrops and Giveaways

- Follow creators on social media
- Participate in community events
- Early supporters may receive free NFTs
- Check newsletter announcements

#### 4. Secondary Market

- Buy from existing holders
- Use peer-to-peer platforms
- Negotiate prices directly
- Transfer via Sui blockchain

### Verifying Your NFT

After acquiring an NFT:

1. **Check Your Wallet:**
   - Open your Sui wallet
   - Go to NFTs section
   - Verify the NFT is there

2. **Test Access:**
   - Go to the newsletter
   - Try to unlock premium content
   - If it works, your NFT is valid!

3. **Troubleshooting:**
   - Ensure NFT is in the connected wallet
   - Verify it's the correct collection
   - Try reconnecting your wallet

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### "No Access" Message Despite Owning NFT

**Solutions:**
1. Verify the NFT is in your connected wallet
2. Check you're connected with the correct wallet
3. Reconnect your wallet
4. Refresh the page
5. Verify the NFT collection address matches

#### Premium Content Won't Decrypt

**Solutions:**
1. Check your internet connection
2. Try unlocking again
3. Clear browser cache
4. Try a different browser
5. Verify Seal key servers are online (check status page)

#### "Transaction Failed" When Subscribing

**Solutions:**
1. Check you have enough SUI for gas fees
2. Verify wallet is connected
3. Try again in a few moments
4. Check Sui network status

#### Content Not Loading

**Solutions:**
1. Check internet connection
2. Refresh the page
3. Try a different browser
4. Clear browser cache
5. Check if Walrus storage is accessible

#### Wallet Won't Connect

**Solutions:**
1. Ensure wallet extension is installed and unlocked
2. Try a different wallet provider
3. Clear browser cache and cookies
4. Update wallet extension
5. Try a different browser

## Privacy and Security

### Your Privacy

**Reading Public Content:**
- No tracking or analytics
- No account required
- Anonymous browsing

**Reading Premium Content:**
- Wallet address is visible on-chain
- Decryption happens locally in your browser
- Content is never sent to servers
- Your reading habits are private

### Security Best Practices

**Wallet Security:**
- Never share your private keys or recovery phrase
- Use hardware wallets for valuable NFTs
- Verify transaction details before approving
- Keep wallet software updated

**Phishing Protection:**
- Always verify the platform URL
- Don't click suspicious links
- Creators will never ask for your private keys
- Be cautious of fake NFT offers

**NFT Safety:**
- Verify NFT collection addresses
- Use reputable marketplaces
- Check creator authenticity
- Be wary of too-good-to-be-true offers
